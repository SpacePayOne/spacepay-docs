# What is SpacePay

SpacePay is a comprehensive cryptocurrency payment platform that enables businesses to accept digital currency payments seamlessly. We provide the infrastructure, tools, and support needed to integrate crypto payments into your existing business operations.

## Supported Chains and Currencies

SpacePay supports a wide range of blockchain networks and digital assets:

### Supported Networks

- **Ethereum** - ETH, USDC, USDT
- **Binance Smart Chain** - BNB, USDC, USDT
- **Polygon** - POL, USDC, USDT
- **Base** - ETH, USDC, USDT
- **Avalanche** - AVAX, USDC, USDT

<Note>
  We continuously add support for new networks and assets. Contact us to request
  support for additional cryptocurrencies.
</Note>

## How SpacePay Works

SpacePay operates as a payment processor that handles the complexity of cryptocurrency transactions while providing you with a simple, familiar payment experience.

<Steps>
<Step title="Merchant Integration">
  Integrate SpacePay into your website or application using our APIs or hosted checkout solutions.
</Step>

<Step title="Customer Payment">
  Customers pay using their preferred cryptocurrency through your checkout flow.
</Step>

<Step title="Transaction Processing">
  SpacePay handles blockchain interactions, confirmations, and currency
  conversion automatically.
</Step>

<Step title="Settlement">
  Receive funds in your preferred currency with transparent fees and fast settlement times.
</Step>
</Steps>
