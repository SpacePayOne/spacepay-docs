# Getting Started with SpacePay

This guide will walk you through creating your SpacePay account and completing the business verification process required to start accepting cryptocurrency payments.

## Create an Account

Getting started with SpacePay is straightforward. Follow these steps to create your merchant account:

<Steps>
<Step title="Visit the Dashboard">
  Go to the [SpacePay Dashboard](https://dashboard.spacepay.co.uk/signup) and click **"Sign Up"**.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter Business Information">
  Fill in the required information:
  - **Company Name**: Your registered business name
  - **Industry**: Select your business industry category
  - **Country**: Your business location
  - **Business Email**: Use your business email address
  - **Phone Number**: Business contact number
  - **Website**: Your company website URL
  - **Logo**: 100x100px (PNG or SVG)
  
  <Tip>
  You can sign up using your Google account for faster registration, or use your business email address.
  </Tip>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Business Onboarding and Verification

SpacePay requires business verification (KYB - Know Your Business) to comply with financial regulations and ensure secure operations.

### Required Documents

Before starting the verification process, gather these documents:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Business Registration" icon="file-text">
  - Certificate of Incorporation
  - Articles of Organization
  - Business License
  - Partnership Agreement (if applicable)
</Card>

<Card title="Identity Verification" icon="id-card">
  - Government-issued photo ID
  - Passport or Driver's License
  - Must be of an authorized signatory
</Card>

<Card title="Address Verification" icon="map-marker">
  - Utility bill (within 3 months)
  - Bank statement
  - Lease agreement
  - Government correspondence
</Card>

<Card title="Business Address" icon="building">
  - Business registration address
  - Operating address (if different)
  - Proof of business location
</Card>
</CardGroup>

### Verification Process

<Steps>
<Step title="Wait for Support Contact">
  Once you've completed your account registration, our support team will contact
  you to gather additional KYC (Know Your Customer) and KYB (Know Your Business)
  information.
</Step>

<Step title="Provide Required Information">
  Our support team will guide you through providing:
   - Business registration documents
   - Identity verification for authorized signatories
    - Additional compliance information
    - Business activity details
</Step>

<Step title="Verification Review">
  Our compliance team will review your information and complete the verification
  process.
  
  <Info>
  You'll receive email updates about your verification status throughout the
  process.
  </Info>
</Step>
</Steps>

### Verification Timeline

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Standard Processing (1-3 business days)">
  Most applications are processed within 1-3 business days. You'll receive email updates about your verification status.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Additional Review (3-7 business days)">
  Some applications may require additional review. This is normal for: - New
  business types - High-risk industries - Complex ownership structures -
  International businesses
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Document Requests">
  If additional documents are needed, you'll receive an email with specific requirements. Respond promptly to avoid delays.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Issues and Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Document Quality Issues">
  **Problem**: Documents are rejected due to poor quality.
  
  **Solution**: 
  - Use good lighting when taking photos
  - Ensure text is clearly readable
  - Avoid shadows or glare
  - Upload original documents when possible
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Business Name Mismatch">
  **Problem**: Business name on documents doesn't match your application.
  **Solution**: - Ensure consistency across all documents - Use your registered
  business name exactly as it appears on official documents - Provide additional
  documentation if names have changed
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Address Verification">
  **Problem**: Address documents are not accepted.
  
  **Solution**:
  - Use documents dated within the last 3 months
  - Ensure the address matches your business registration
  - Provide multiple documents if available
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
