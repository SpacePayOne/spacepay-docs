# Compliance and Security

SpacePay maintains the highest standards of compliance and security to protect your business and customers.

## KYB Requirements

Know Your Business (KYB) verification is required for all merchants using SpacePay. This process helps us comply with financial regulations and protect against fraud.

<Card title="Why KYB is Required" icon="shield-alt">
  **Regulatory Compliance** - KYB helps us comply with anti-money laundering
  (AML) and counter-terrorism financing (CTF) regulations. **Risk Management** -
  We verify business identity and assess risk profiles to prevent fraudulent
  activities. **Customer Protection** - KYB protects your business reputation
  and customer funds.
</Card>

### KYB Process

<Steps>
<Step title="Account Creation">
  Provide basic business information during signup:
  - Company name and type
  - Business address
  - Contact information
</Step>

<Step title="Support Contact">
  Our support team will contact you to collect additional KYC/KYB information
  and required documents.
</Step>

<Step title="Verification Review">
  Our compliance team reviews your information and documents for approval.
</Step>

<Step title="Account Activation">
  Once approved, your account is activated and you can start processing payments.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Processing Time**: KYB verification typically takes 1-3 business days.
  Contact our support team if you have questions about the process.
</Note>
